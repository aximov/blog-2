## Career Journey Overview

### First Year
- Started at a small company with four employees
- Chose this company over a larger, reputable one for more work control
- Worked with senior colleagues
- Struggled to define career direction

### Second Year
- Continued feeling uncertain
- Gave "locally optimal answers in the dark"
- Received lower-than-expected performance evaluation

### Third Year
- Began understanding company's role in capitalist economy
- Developed sense of professional responsibility
- Explored product and project management
- Read books on management, Agile, and user experience
- Conducted initial user research

## Future Goals
Current objective: "Demonstrate servant leadership in product and project management while doing development"

Potential key results for fourth year:
- Learn three new programming languages
- Create a hobby service
- Read ten computer science books
- Write ten articles about learned knowledge
- Successfully manage three projects

## Personal Reflection
I acknowledge not yet seeing the ultimate long-term goal, but feel more clarity about near-term professional development.
